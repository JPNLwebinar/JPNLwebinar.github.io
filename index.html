Japan-Netherlands Life Science webinars

Since international travelling and in-person meetings remain restricted, students and early-carrier scientists may have less chance to communicate science and establish connections with the international community. Thus, we launched a series of monthly online seminars, where students and postdocs from several institutions in Japan and Netherlands will present, discuss and have some time for the informal discussions.

The main theme of these webinars will be Microbial Chemical Ecology and related fields.
